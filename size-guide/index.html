Size Guide

Find your perfect fit with our comprehensive size guide

Getting the right size is essential for the perfect fit. Our size guide will help you determine which size is best for you. All measurements are in inches unless otherwise specified.

How to Measure

For the most accurate measurements, use a soft measuring tape and follow these steps:

Chest

Measure around the fullest part of your chest, keeping the tape measure horizontal.

Waist

Measure around your natural waistline, keeping one finger between the tape and your body.

Hips

Measure around the fullest part of your hips and bottom.

Inseam

Measure from your crotch to the bottom of your ankle.
