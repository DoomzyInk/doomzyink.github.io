Refund Policy

Last updated: June 10, 2025

Last Updated: June 10, 2025

At Doomzy Ink, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not entirely happy with your order, we're here to help. Please review our refund policy below.

Important Notice

All clearance, sale items, and personalized/custom products are final sale and not eligible for return or refund unless they arrive damaged or defective. Please review your order carefully before completing your purchase.

1. Eligibility for Refunds

To be eligible for a refund, your item must be:

  • In its original condition (unworn, unwashed, and with all tags attached)
  • In the original packaging
  • Accompanied by the original receipt or proof of purchase
  • Returned within 30 days of the delivery date

2. Non-Refundable Items

The following items are not eligible for return or refund:

  • Clearance or sale items (marked as "Final Sale")
  • Personalized or custom-made products
  • Gift cards
  • Items that have been worn, washed, or altered
  • Items without the original tags attached

3. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please follow these steps:

Step 1

Contact our customer service team at returns@doomzyink.com within 30 days of receiving your order to initiate the return process.

Step 2

Package the item(s) securely in the original packaging, including all tags and accessories. Include a copy of your order confirmation or receipt.

Step 3

Ship the package to the address provided in your return authorization email. We recommend using a trackable shipping method, as we cannot be responsible for lost returns.

4. Refund Processing

Once we receive your return, we will inspect it and notify you that we've received it. We will process your refund within 3-5 business days after receiving and inspecting the returned item(s).

Refund Methods

  • Credit/Debit Card Purchases: Refunds will be issued to the original payment method within 5-10 business days after processing.
  • PayPal Purchases: Refunds will be issued to your PayPal account within 1-2 business days after processing.
  • Store Credit: If you prefer, you may request store credit instead of a refund to your original payment method.

5. Return Shipping

Customers are responsible for return shipping costs, unless the return is due to our error (e.g., you received an incorrect or defective item). We recommend using a trackable shipping service and purchasing shipping insurance, as we cannot guarantee that we will receive your returned item.

6. Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact us immediately at support@doomzyink.com within 7 days of delivery. Please include your order number and photos of the damaged or defective item. We will provide a prepaid return label and process a replacement or refund once we receive the returned item.

7. Late or Missing Refunds

If you haven't received your refund within the expected timeframe, please:

  1. Check your bank account or credit card statement again
  2. Contact your credit card company or bank, as it may take some time for the refund to be officially posted
  3. If you've done all of this and still have not received your refund, please contact us at support@doomzyink.com

8. Exchanges

We currently do not offer direct exchanges. If you need a different size or color, please return the original item for a refund and place a new order for the desired item.

9. International Returns

International customers are responsible for all return shipping costs and any applicable customs fees or import duties. We recommend declaring the package as a "Returned Goods" to avoid additional charges. Refunds will be issued in US Dollars and may be subject to currency exchange rates and fees.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our refund policy, please contact us at:

Email: support@doomzyink.com

Hours: Monday-Friday, 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM EST
