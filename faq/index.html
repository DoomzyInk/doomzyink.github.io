To ensure your Doomzy Ink clothing stays looking its best, we recommend the following care instructions:

Washing:

  • Machine wash cold with like colors
  • Use mild detergent
  • Turn garments inside out before washing
  • Wash dark colors separately

Drying:

  • Tumble dry low or lay flat to dry
  • Avoid high heat to prevent shrinking
  • Remove promptly from dryer to prevent wrinkles

Ironing: If needed, iron on reverse side using low heat. Avoid direct contact with printed designs.
